# Enterprise CRM System  
UI/UX Prototype & Full Stack Application Development

---

## Executive Summary

This repository contains the design and development work for an **Enterprise-level Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system**. The project is initiated with a complete **Adobe XD UI/UX prototype**, designed using a **real-world business workflow and design thinking methodology**.

The system is being developed as a **scalable, role-based CRM platform**, focusing on customer management, service ticket handling, and operational workflows across multiple user roles.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to design and implement a **centralized CRM solution** that improves:

- Customer engagement and service efficiency  
- Ticket lifecycle management  
- Role-based operational control  
- Transparency between support and service teams  

---

## Core Capabilities

### Role-Based Access
- **Admin Dashboard** – System control, user management, analytics
- **Support Executive Dashboard** – Ticket creation and monitoring
- **Technician Dashboard** – Task assignment and service execution
- **Customer Portal** – Ticket tracking and communication

### Functional Modules
- Customer profile management
- Ticket creation, assignment, and resolution workflow
- Service tracking and status updates
- Dashboard-driven workflow visibility
- Scalable and modular UI architecture

---

## Design & UX Strategy

- Design thinking approach
- Real-world enterprise CRM use case
- User-centric interface design
